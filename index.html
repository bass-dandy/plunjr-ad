What is Plunjr?

Plunjr collects and displays public restroom reviews left by users for users. These reviews improve the public restroom experience and can even help you make an informed decision in a time of need. Plunjr also helps you get where you're going on time. Turn by turn directions to each restroom are a touch away.

Features:

  • Rate and review public restrooms
  • Find all reviewed restrooms in your area
  • See photos and read reviews left by other users
  • Get turn by turn directions to a public restroom (powered by Google Maps)
